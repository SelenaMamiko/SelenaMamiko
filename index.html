Travel to ....

A wonderful place called Wonderland. It is on a stranded land mass in the sky. Beautiful plants, caterpillars, rabbits, and cats are all over this island!

There is a lot to do on this island and many people to meet! Not many understand the ways here, so you need to visit it to believe it!

What it has.....

  • Giant Mushrooms
  • Running Rabbits
  • Tea Parties
  • Crazy Wildlife like disappearing cats!

How to get there....

  1. Find a hole near a tree
  2. Go head first into it
  3. Fall some time and you are there!
